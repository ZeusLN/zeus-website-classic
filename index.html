Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

Nothing. Zeus is 100% open source and free.

What information do you collect?

We do not collect any usage or analytics information when you view this website or when you use Zeus on your phone, with the exception of the crash reports that the app stores provide. Your privacy is incredibly important to us.

I connected Zeus to my node but the balances incorrectly display as 0

Make sure you connect Zeus to your node's REST service. Other lightning wallets are known to use GRPC instead.

Can I use it on mainnet or testnet?

Yep, you use Zeus on mainnet, testnet, or regtest.

Can I contribute to the project?
  • Any feedback you have would be greatly appreciated. Let us know via email, Telegram, or Twitter.
  • Downloaded the app from Google Play? Please leave us a review.
  • If you know Typescript and React then pull requests on GitHub are a great way to give back and help shape the product.

Still have a question?

Please head on over to the project's GitHub repo to let us know what's on your mind.
